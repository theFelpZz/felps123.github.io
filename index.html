What is it?

It is a full-featured WhatsApp bot based on a character I'm really fan of, which is called Bigby Wolf from the comics “Fables”. (Oh and I strongly recommend you to play the game, it's amazing! Telltale Games❤️).

Do I have to pay for it?

No! It's completely free and there are no limits. But you can help me keep it running by donating any amount.

Some of the available features

Command What can it do?
!help Sends a list with all available features.
!sticker Turns any image/video or gif into a sticker!
!google Don't know something? Well, just google it!
!image This is quite useful when you need wallpapers or something like that.
!ris Want to find the source of an image or even similar images? Well, this can help you with that!
!whatsong Identifies music just by listening to a sample of their audio.
!lyrics Finds the lyrics for any song.
!mp3 A very fast and efficient music downloader.
!mp4 Works just like !mp3, but it sends you videos instead.
!book -download Do you like reading? If yes then you're going to love this!
!torrent I guess you can already tell what it does just by its name, it's a torrent search engine!
!define An English dictionary, this can come in handy sometimes.
